Our Commitment to Privacy

The Give For Good Global Foundation respects the privacy of every visitor to our websites and every user of our mobile apps. Your privacy is a priority, and we go to great lengths to protect it. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect and use information about our online visitors. We know you care about your information and we want you to be fully informed about our practices.

This Policy may be changed or updated from time to time. By visiting our website or using our mobile app, you accept the practices described in this Policy.

The Data We Collect

Our website and mobile app provide convenient ways for you to access our services and learn about important Give For Good Global opportunities and events. We collect information about our online visitors both directly from our visitors and indirectly from other companies that provide data to us. We use this information to communicate with our supporters and clients and process their transactions. We do not sell it to other organizations.

When you use our website or app to make a financial contribution, register for a class or special event, sign up to volunteer, apply for a job, or book an event, we may ask you for your name, address, phone number(s), and other information relevant to the transaction. We will ask for standard credit card information when you make a financial donation, pay for a class, or make a purchase. We obtain information about our online visitors from other sources as well. For example, we engage vendors that provide contact information and other data. We use this data to help us engage with people who may be interested in participating in Give For Good Global activities or contributing to one of our missions.
