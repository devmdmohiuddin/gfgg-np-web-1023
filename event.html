Fundraise For Give For Good Global Fundation

Let your friends and family know that you are fundraising for GFGG to help meet the urgent needs of people enduring poverty, disaster, violent conflict, and the acute impacts of climate change. Every dollar you raise can help send food, water, shelter, and lifesaving relief to communities facing the world's toughest challenges.

It's easy to get started:

  • Fill out the registration form below to create your campaign page.
  • Customize your page by adding your story (optional).
  • Then you're ready to spread the word and invite friends, colleagues, and family to donate.
  • We will be here to help you along the way! (If you have any questions, please email info@giveforgoodglobal.org for assistance).
